The year is 2352. Civilisation has been destroyed, Governments have fallen, Chaos is now control.

The world as we know it has been destroyed by the mech weilding Omega-cult, a malicious group of cyborg cultists
that manipulate technology to their will and use it to force their ideals on others.

The Great War has scarred what remains of humanity but the battle-hardened people of New Plymstone have had enough.
